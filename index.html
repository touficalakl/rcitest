Leading IT Solutions for Your Business

Overview and History

Relational Computer International (RCI) was established in 1993 and has consistently been active in the IT sector. Since its foundation, RCI has partnered with Sage and INGRES as solution providers, and with CA since 1996. In 2006, the company shifted its strategic focus, transitioning into a comprehensive solutions provider working with a diverse range of vendors to meet market demands, adding both hardware and software solutions to its portfolio.

Leadership and Expertise

Led by seasoned professionals with over 30 years of experience in the IT industry, RCI aims to be one of the top IT solution provider in Lebanon and the Levant. The company is committed to offering cutting-edge enterprise solutions, backed by expert services, to help clients optimize their operations.

IT Evolution

The rapid growth of the internet and online business, along with traditional business needs, has sped up the development of enterprise technology. RCI uses its experience to help with this change, providing smart solutions that help businesses improve their operations and serve their customers better.
